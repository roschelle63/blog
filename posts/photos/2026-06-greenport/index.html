A visit to Greenport

June 9, 2026 · 1 min · Jeremy Roschelle

Audrey and I visited Greenport with Aunt Grace on a beautiful June morning. We visited the cute shops, watched the ferry in the harbor, and bought some books at Burton’s. Then we had an amazing lunch at First and South, which specializes in Gluten-Free cooking.

While the ladies were shopping, I snapped some photos of Greenport’s signs. Signs of a different time. Tap any image for larger photos.
