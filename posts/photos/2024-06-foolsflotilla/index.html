Fools' Flotilla: A Madison Tradition

June 9, 2024 · 1 min · Jeremy Roschelle

A hot tip led us to Madison’s Yahara River for the annual Fools’ Flotilla. This boat parade flowed down the river between Madison’s two lakes on a beautiful Sunday morning. The end destination was the Marquette Neighborhood Association’s Waterfront Festival. Good zany fun.
