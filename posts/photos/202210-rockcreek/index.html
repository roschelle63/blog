Fall in Rock Creek

October 30, 2022 · 1 min · Jeremy Roschelle

Fall is breaking out all over, and no where better then nearby Rock Creek Park. We love that Beach Dr is closed to cars; its the perfect weekend stroll. And because of the absense of cars anywhere nearby, it’s possible to experience complete quiet even though we’re in middle of DC.

A couple of weekends ago, we were walking here and Audrey spotted Justice Sonia Sotomayor walking by. A DC moment!

Enjoy the photos below, and you can click to make them full screen. I was having fun looking for places where the reflections in the water were also gold and red.
