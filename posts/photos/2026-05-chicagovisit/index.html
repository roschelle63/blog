Chicago!

May 16, 2026 · 1 min · Jeremy Roschelle

Audrey and I visited Gene and Laem for a perfect Mother’s Day weekend. On our first day, we walked the Gold Coast and enjoyed a jazz show. On “architecture day,” we enjoyed the boat tour and then went out to Oak Park to see Frank LLoyd Wright’s house and some of his designs. We also enjoyed the most amazing Thai meal, by Chef Laem. Chicago is a great city!
