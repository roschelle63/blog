Old La Honda Redwoods

December 30, 2025 · 1 min · Jeremy Roschelle

It’s been a relaxing winter break. One day Audrey and I took Ollie for a walk in a small redwood canyon called Thornewood Preserve, which is two miles from our house. I liked it so much I decided to come back the next day with my camera.

I brought a tripod along, so that I could take long exposure shots and make the water look smooth and glassy. It’s quite dark in the forest and the winter sun was very low. It’s quite hard to photograph a redwood forest, but these seemed to capture the spirit.
