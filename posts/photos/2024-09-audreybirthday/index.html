Audrey's Birthday in Madison

September 22, 2024 · 2 min · Jeremy Roschelle

We celebrated Audrey’s 60th Birthday with a gathering of friends and family in Madison, Wisconsin. A weekend of fun!

Friday#

We started the morning with Molly and Jeff, who were first to arrive. We enjoyed a delicious breakfast at Marigold Kitchen, across from the state capitol. After a walk to see Audrey’s office in WisDems HQ, we toured the capitol which has spectacular marble and is generally grand.

Then we went apple picking. After a briefing and tasting of all the varieties in the orchard, we picked some jonamac and cortland apples. Yum!

The family soon arrived and so did Kamala Harris. A highlight of the weekend was attending her Madison Rally. There’s a separate blog about the rally.

For dinner that night, we went to nearby Sardine, a french bistro.

Saturday#

Everyone stayed in the Embassy Suites, which treated us all to breakfast. Then we toured the famous Dane County Farmer’s Market and picked up supplies.

Sarah made amazing t-shirts for all, featuring the “Notorious ASG”—Audrey as her favorite Supreme.

We all wore the shirts for a pontoon boat ride on Lake Mendota, traveling across the lake to a picnic. Of course, the picnic featured our purchases from the farmer’s market. After the picnic, we saw a storm approaching from the west. Fortunately, our dock was to the east. Our captain made a run for it, and we made it back before the rain.

The big dinner was at a restaurant called Pig in a Fur Coat. We got the chef’s tasting menu and dishes just kept coming; lots of things to taste and enjoy. The guests each shared special stories for Audrey. A warm, loving conclusion to a extended weekend together.

Thanks to all who came to celebrate with Audrey!
