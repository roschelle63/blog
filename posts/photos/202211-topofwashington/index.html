Top of Washington Monument

November 8, 2022 · 1 min · Jeremy Roschelle

As the culmination of a great family visit with Amy, Len, Isla and Jack, we toured the Washington Monument. We were blessed with a very clear day and great views in every direction. After passing through security (some guy failed to get through with a large purple scissors), we were whisked 90 seconds up in an elevator to the top.

Jefferson Memorial and the Tidal Basin (South)#

The Potomac River flows out beyond the memorial.

National Mall & the Capitol (East)#

Smithsonian buildings parade along the Mall towards the Capitol, flanked by the bastions of bureaucracy.

White House (North)#

We live in this direction, about 2 miles north of the White House. I still love that I can ride a bike to the White House about 20 minutes.

Lincoln Memorial (West)#

The World War II Memorial is in the front, followed by the Reflecting Pool, and then the Lincoln Monument

We went to see Uncle Abe the night before; he was looking good.

The Tour Ends#

We really had a wonderful visit with Amy, Len, Jack and Isla (and Ollie will be especially said not to have Isla around). We had a great time doing things together in the capital.
